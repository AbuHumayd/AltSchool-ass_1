One of my beloved western Islamic Scholars

Abu Taymiyyah is a distinguished Islamic scholar and daee, widely respected for his deep understanding of Qur’anic teachings and Sunnah. Hailing from the United Kingdom, he embarked on a transformative journey to Yemen to immerse himself in Islamic studies. There, he dedicated himself to memorizing the Qur’an and delving into the intricate nuances of Islamic jurisprudence (fiqh), under the guidance of esteemed scholars. Following his enriching experience in Yemen, Ustadh Abu Taymiyyah continued his academic journey at the renowned Islamic University of Madinah. During his time in Madinah, he had the privilege of studying alongside prominent scholars such as Shaykh Suleyman Ruheili and Shaykh Abdurazaq Al Badar, solidifying his expertise in Islamic law and spirituality.
